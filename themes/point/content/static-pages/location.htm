Location



Address: The station for Entrepreneurship.


Baghdad – Alkarrada – 903 District


The station for entrepreneurship is located in the heart of the capital, Baghdad, in the Karrada area, near Uqba bin Nafie Square.


The station is the first entrepreneurial project to provide a common working space in Iraq. The station was built not only to provide the physical space needed by entrepreneurs in Iraq to launch and build their business. It also works to provide a supportive society, a healthy environment and a comprehensive organizational environment. Enabling entrepreneurs and technicians to meet together in the first common space for creative development in Baghdad.


The aim is to help aspiring entrepreneurs in Iraq plan and launch their own projects by providing them with the resources, space, and vital support needed to enable entrepreneurial projects to gain control and success without hindrance. Although the station is an instrument found in physical space, it is also a symbolic gesture representing the most important stage in the initiation of any action (the initial stage).


The station is one of the few places open to all to make the future.
